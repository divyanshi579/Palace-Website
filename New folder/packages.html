2 Nights 3 Days Group Package

ITENARY

Day 01 : Check in ,Half day sightseeing and bonfire Pick up from Cochin International Airport/railway station.Check in to Chithirapuram Palace by 1 PM Have lunch from our restaurant and go for half day sightseeing covering Chengulam Dam boating,Chithirapuram view point , nearby view points and waterfalls. Come back to the bungalow and enjoy bonfire . Have dinner at our restaurant and overnight stay.

Day 02: Full day Munnar sightseeing After breakfast go for full day sightseeing in and around Munnar covering Hydel park,Eravikulam national park ( were the Neelakurinji flowers blooms ) ,Mattupetty dam,Photo points, Honey Bee Net,Shooting points,Flower Garden visit and Tea Museum visit . Come back to the Palace in the evening . and have dinner at our restaurant .

Day 03: After breakfast check out from the resort and drop to Airport/Railway station or bus station.

Check in time: 12 PM

Check out time: 11 AM

3 Nights 4 Days Package

ITENARY

Enjoy Cultural program or go to near by Adventure parks (with own expense ). Come back to the Palace and enjoy Bonfire . Candle light dinner will be provided as per your choice ( in your room/ at our restaurant /open area ) . Overnight stay

Day 02: Full day Munnar sightseeing , Candlelight dinner and flower bed decoration After breakfast go for full day sightseeing in and around Munnar covering Hydel Park ,Eravikulam National Park ( were the Neelakurinji flowers blooms ) ,Mattupetty Dam , Photo points , Honey Bee Net, Shooting points, Flower Garden visit and Tea Museum visit . Come back to the palace and have Candlelight dinner along with honeymoon cake . Your bed will be decorated with flowers so enjoy the night stay.

Day 03: Full day sight seeing in Munnar After breakfast go for a walk inside Tea Garden and Soft trekking to the nearby mountain . Go for sightseeing covering Chengulam dam boating, Attukad water falls, Karadippara view point, photo points, Tea garden visit etc.. Come back to the Palace enjoy bonfire, candlelight dinner .Badam milk will be provided in the room . Overnight stay

Day 04: Check out and drop After breakfast check out from the palace and proceed to Cochin airport/railway station / bus station to catch your bus.
